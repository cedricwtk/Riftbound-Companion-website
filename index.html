Match Score Tracker

Real-time scoring for Best-of-1 and Best-of-3 matches. Pick your legend and battlefield, tap to score, and let the app track game wins, determine the series winner, and save the full match record automatically.

Card Database Browser

Search the full Riftbound card pool by name, domain (Fury, Body, Calm, Mind, Order, Chaos), card type, and rarity. Tap any card for its full art, cost, power, rules text, and flavor text.

Build decks with full rule enforcement — 1 legend, 1 champion, up to 3 battlefields, 12 runes, 40 main deck cards, and an 8-card sideboard. Domain-locked filtering keeps only legal cards visible. Export your deck in UVS format, ready to share and play immediately at any Skirmish event.

Real Use Cases

Built for Real Moments at the Table

Every feature was designed around how Riftbound players actually play, build, and trade.

Match Score Tracker

Score any match, at any table

Two friends sit down at a local game store for a Best-of-3. They open Riftbound Companion, pick their legends and a battlefield, and tap to score points. The app tracks individual game wins within the series, determines the overall winner, and saves the full match record automatically.

  • Best-of-1 & Best-of-3 formats
  • Configurable win conditions (7, 8, 9, or 10 points)
  • Legend & battlefield picker with searchable database
  • Haptic feedback · Auto-save

Match History & Analytics

Review every game you've played

A player has been grinding matches all week and wants to see how they're performing. They open match history, filter by wins to review their best games, check their overall win rate, and edit a match from yesterday where they accidentally selected the wrong legend.

  • Chronological match log with win/loss indicators
  • Win rate calculation & display
  • Filter by outcome (wins, losses, all)
  • Edit & delete past matches Premium

Card Database Browser

Look up any card, instantly

A player hears about a card during a stream and wants to look it up. They search by name, find it in seconds, and tap for full art, cost, power, rules text, and flavor text. Another player wants to browse all Epic Fury spells to find options for a new deck idea.

  • Search by name, domain, type, and rarity
  • Full card detail view with art & flavor text
  • Paginated browsing (20 cards per page)
  • 24-hour local cache for offline access

Deck Builder & Analytics

Build and analyze your decks

A player wants to build a new deck around a specific legend. The app filters the card pool to only cards in that legend's domains. They pick a champion, add battlefields and runes, fill out the main deck and sideboard — then pull up analytics to review the rune curve and power distribution before a tournament.

  • Full deck rule enforcement (legend, champion, battlefields, runes)
  • Domain-locked card filtering
  • Create, rename, and delete decks
  • Export in UVS format — ready to play at Skirmish events
  • Deck analytics: curve, power, type & domain Premium

Trade Manager

Never miss an in-person trade

Two players agree on a card trade at a local meetup next Saturday. One player creates a trade entry with the other player's name, adds the cards being exchanged, sets the meetup date, time, and location, and notes the agreed price. The app schedules a push notification reminder for the day before. When they're ready to meet, they share a trade summary card — as an image or text — so both parties have the details confirmed.

  • Cards, trader name, meetup details, price & notes
  • Push notification reminder one day before
  • Share as formatted text or styled image card
  • Optional e-transfer email on shared trade cards
  • Trade status tracking: Upcoming, Completed, Cancelled

Works Offline

Card data is cached locally for 24 hours. Browse the full database and use every app feature without a network connection — no signal required at the table.

No Account Required

All match history, decks, and trade data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Nothing is sent to a server. Your data stays yours, on-device only.

Android 8.0+

Lightweight and compatible with most modern Android devices. Free to download with no sign-up, no ads, and no paywalls to get started. Available on Google Play.
